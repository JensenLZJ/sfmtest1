Usage Guidelines

Do's

  • Use the logo at appropriate sizes with adequate clear space
  • Maintain the logo's aspect ratio when scaling
  • Use the correct logo variation for your background (light/dark)
  • Ensure sufficient contrast between logo and background
  • Use brand colors consistently across all materials
  • Respect the minimum clear space around the logo
  • Use high-resolution SVG files for print materials

Don'ts

  • Do not distort, rotate, or modify the logo
  • Do not change the logo colors unless using approved variations
  • Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds that reduce visibility
  • Do not add effects like shadows, outlines, or gradients to the logo
  • Do not use outdated or modified versions of the logo
  • Do not use the logo smaller than the minimum size requirement
