About SamudraFM

Discover our story, mission, and the team behind the music

Our Story

SamudraFM is a recreational school radio initiative at SMK Taman SEA, designed to give students hands-on broadcasting experience while exploring creativity in music, events, and radio production.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a platform for students to express themselves, develop communication skills, and learn about radio production in a fun and engaging environment.

What We Do

  • Live radio broadcasting
  • Music curation and playlist creation
  • Student-led shows and content
  • Community engagement and events
  • Technical skills development
