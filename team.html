Visionary Teacher

The visionary English teacher who asked the transformative question "What if students can own a radio station?" in 2023. This simple yet powerful question sparked the creation of samudrafm, inspiring students to turn an idea into reality, and who served as Teacher in Charge from 2023–2024.

Join Our Team

Interested in becoming part of the samudrafm family? We're always looking for passionate students who want to contribute to our radio program. Whether you're interested in hosting, technical work, content creation, or community management, there's a place for you on our team.

Join Us

Fully volunteer-run radio station from SMK Taman SEA, Petaling Jaya

Our programme is a recreational school radio initiative, designed to give students hands-on broadcasting experience while exploring creativity in music, events, and radio production. Not to use outside of Malaysia.
